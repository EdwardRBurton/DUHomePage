Card Sort Insights 1/27/2018

  • Author: Ed Burton
Card Sort Methododolgy

The 20 lables I created for the cardsort were based upon content in each of the three pages for Pearce Jams. I used an open cardsort which allowed the users to create the categories. It was important to see those categories to ensure the site structure could be refreshed. Both participants had already used the first version of Pearce Jams, which influenced the results.

Results

Here is a link to the Optimal Workshop Card Sort

The cardsort results did not change my approach to the web app. Each participant create different categories for 17 out of the 20 items. The only matching category was for "Color" which makes sense due to the colors used for the app theme. Most of the other categoeies were similar, leading me to not want to make changes to the site structure. This was influenced by the fact my participants had already seen the app.
